Introduction

In my time onboard The Massive, home of the Irken Armada, I took an interest in researching the biology of an Irken. Not much recorded research exists onboard the Massive or possible in even any official Irken records. That made the research very difficult for me, especially as a soldier. Because I was not on any of the Vort Research Stations at any point in time (I was deemed a risk due to 'stupidity' and 'he may blow something up') even less research oportunities existed for me. So i had to study in secret, using my little spare time investing myself in my inventions and biological research. Below is most of the information I have gathered, in no particular order.

the mystery of limbs

In my research, I have discovered that Irkens can grow up to two more pairs of arms, a tail, and perhaps even more sorts of limbs at will. These limbs may have come from the Irken anscestors, who also used to have mandibles and shells. I have reason to believe that Irkens also had Earthen-Insect-like wings. Something notable is that even half-Irkens experience this phenomenon, and Zib seems to have more control over the arms. He does not seem to be able to sprout a tail. All this aside, Irken limb growth has been documented briefly by the scientists on The Massive, but they are not too interested in this, as the PAK and other weaponry innovation takes precedence pver biology and things like that.

Arms

Forelimbs can sprout with immense effort and appear to be normal. The more I learn, the more I will put down. Perhaps I will modify my Uniform in order to accomodate these. It is hard work adjusting to having more limbs than usual, but in the long run, it can be very helpful with my inventions.

Irken Tails.

Just like the two arms Irkens have hidden inside of their body, tails can be grown by an Irken with no abnormalities. Something about tails is that they are seen as 'an act of rebellion or defection'. So most Irkens have never seen a tail in their lives, and those who have view it with scrutiny and distaste. Many Irkens who take part in The Resisty & The Irken Resistance tend to permanently implement their tails into their general appearance. I have done so too, especially because it tends to be more comfortable.
