Irken as a Language

Simplified Irken vs Traditional Irken vs Ancient Irken

There are three versions of the Irken language, but 2/3 use the same alphabet, while Ancient Irken used something similar to hieroglyphics. Below are the differences between the alphabets and the languages. Simplified Irken is often used in intergalactic negotiations and treaties, while Traditional Irken is used between Irkens and is mostly understood solely by Irkens and scholars (plus the occasional lucky human stink-bag!).

Simplified Irken

In Simplified Irken, the only capitalized letters are the letters in an Irken's name. Otherwise, it is very similar to earthen Inglish, just a different alphabet! I have been informed that this constitutes as a symbol substitution cipher.

Translation Example:

hello, my name is ICK and this is simplified irken!

hello, my name is ICK and this is simplified irken!

Ancient Irken

This pronounciation is more complicated, and is very difficult, as it is Ancient Irken (B.E. or Before Empire). There is no formal written version of Ancient Irken, mostly doodles to express needs. The sounds were comparable to English Morse Code, where a dah/dash - chirp/buzz (any longer buggish sound) dit/dots - click (like a bug sound, since irkens are technically space bugs lol)

Traditional Irken

To a non-Irken, Traditional/Modern Irken is very difficult, and can compare to earthen Russia. The grammar is very different and follows a "verb-adjective-subject" structure and OSV word order.
